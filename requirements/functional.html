2. Functional Requirements

The primary functionality of the Scheduler is for generating a schedule of university courses with assigned instructors and rooms. Instructors specify their course and time preferences and scheduling administrators use the data to perform scheduling operations. The Scheduler also provides functionality for scheduling administrators to view and manually edit schedules, along with their data.

Following an overview of the Scheduler user interface, details of system use are presented in the following scenarios:

  • basic schedule generation
  • database management
  • advanced schedule generation and editing
  • viewing the schedule in a variety of ways
  • receiving student feedback
  • server administration
  • details of file-related commands

 

These scenarios cover all functional requirements for the system.
