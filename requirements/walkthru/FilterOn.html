Schedule View - Filters

This view displays the various options for filtering the table representing the schedule. Each checkbox labeled with a day of the week toggles between showing the day of the week in the schedule view. When toggled on, the day of the week will display the classes of that day. While off, the table will resize to show the remaining selected days of the week.

The drop down tree expands and collapses options to toggle on and off various teachers, rooms, and classes.
