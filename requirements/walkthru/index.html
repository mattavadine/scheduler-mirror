Welcome to the prototype walkthrough of the Cal Poly scheduling system.
Use the index below to view the mockups. While you explore, please keep in mind that this is a prototype. Please provide any feedback you have to the scheduler forum, located at http://scheduler.csc.calpoly.edu/forum/
