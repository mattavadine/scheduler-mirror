2.3.1.3. Remove Instructor

If an instructor is selected and then the Remove button is clicked, this popup will appear.



Figure 2.3.1.3a. Remove Instructor Confirmation window

For greater certainty that the correct instructor was selected, this popup will include the instructor's ID.
Click Yes to finalize the removal or No to go back.
