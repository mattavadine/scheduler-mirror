Filter View

This is the dialog relating to filtering the contents of the table in the schedule viewer. The 5 check boxes represent each day of the week shown in the schedule view table. When checked, the day of the week will show up in the schedule view. When not checked, that day will be filter out from the view leaving only the checked days left. One box must be checked at all times and will be uncheckable if 4 days of the week are currently not checked. The 3 lists represent all the different filtering options for instructors, rooms, and courses. By checking on and off the various options in the list, the corresponding schedule table will update accordingly. If all selections are checked off, then the schedule view will be completely blank.
