2.3.1. Instructor

In this view, an administrator will see a list of instructors. The user can view a preview of information on an instructor by clicking on the instructor's name in the list on the left; the instructor's information will be on the right.



Figure 2.3.1a. Instructor Management window

To add an instructor, click on the Add button.
To edit an instructor, select an instructor from the list on the left and then click on the Edit button.
To remove and instructor, select an instructor from the list on the left and then click on the Remove button.
