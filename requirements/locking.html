2.4.3.2 Locking

An administrator can lock a desired course. When a desired course is locked Schedule generation will not affect the locked course. This means the the courses instructor, time, and location will remain the same before and after a schedule has been modified by schedule generation or conflict resolution.

The user can select a course to lock by click the checkbox near the course shown in figure 48.
