Editorial Notes for CSC 402: The requirements table of contents has been retained from the Liebowitz project. It is definitely subject to change in the 2011-12 402 project. The structure of Section 1 is likely to remain stable for the 402 project. Subsection 1.5 will contain the integrated and edited versions of this year's 402 tool reviews, as well as recent past tool reviews that were not reviewed this year in 308. Section 2.1 is most likely to be retained, but the remaining Section 2 organization will almost certainly change for this year's project.
