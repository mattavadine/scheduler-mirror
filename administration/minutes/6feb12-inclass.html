  2. Notes on the Instructor UI:

    1. Can do two things: enter prefs and view a schedule; zero or more schedules may be viewable at any one time.

    2. Need to be clear about the following:

      1. What does the admin press to publish a schedule? Publish? This still needs to be answered.

      1. Home

      2. Archive

      3. Trash

      Home   Settings                                   Archive   Trash
      I.e., 'Archive' and 'Trash' are on the far right of the tab bar.
