Spring Week 8 Acceptance Test Report for the MU Department


Browser Used for Testing: Used Sellenium Testing for data entry with Chrome



1. Create, Save, and Re-Open a New Schedule

Able to login using "admin" and create, save and re-open the schedule.


2. Enter Resource Data

Location and Instructor entry was solid.

Issues
  • Day Combinations were not 100%. When I modified the SCU, a red checkmark appeared next to my day combination selection

3. Generate a Schedule

Unable to generate a schedule using the "Generate Schedule" button. Loading gif came up after pressing the button, and a 500 error popped up.


4. Edit the Schedule

Moving courses around on the schedule with drag and drop was successful.

Issues
  • When dragging a course onto the schedule, it is unclear how many hours a week the course is supposed to be scheduled and what day combinations are valid. The window that pops up should have more info about the course I am manually placing on the schedule.

5. Save, Exit, Re-Open Schedule

Saving worked (nice popup). When I logged out and back in, I was able to see the same schedule and open it back up again.


6. Quality Measures

Generally the product looks good. In random user tests, the look and feel was functional/familiar.

Issues
  • Location of the Generate Schedule button is hard for most people to find
  • The schedulable checkbox confused users since they thought it was the way to indicate which rows were selected. They tried to delete resources by checking the box and clicking remove

7. Other Issues

  • After running Selenium tests with MU resources, Generate Schedule button caused a 500 error after entering resources.
  • After entering 2 courses and no instructors/locations, clicking Generate Schedule caused infinite loading gif.
  • Logged out and back in, chose same schedule, clicked generate. Loading gif came up and disappeared, but nothing was on the schedule.
